C (Contact) -- degree of encounter with reality. C = I(X; T_B(Y)).
A (Agenda) -- post-channel distortion. A = I(X; T_B(Y)) - I(X; f(T_B(Y))).
A Decomposition: A = A_bias + A_capacity. A_bias is motivated distortion. A_capacity is unmotivated limitation. Both >= 0. Axiom 2 applies to both.

Axiom 3 (Substrate Independence). For exact functor F between categories with recognition structures, the image of the exact sequence is exact.
